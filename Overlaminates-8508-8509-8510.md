# 3M Scotchcal Overlaminates

## Product Name
3M Scotchcal Overlaminates 8508, 8509, 8510

## Description
3M Scotchcal Overlaminates are durable, protective films designed to enhance and prolong the life of printed graphics. These overlaminates provide a glossy finish and safeguard against scratches, fading, and environmental factors, ensuring your graphics look vibrant for longer.

## Features
- **Durability**: Offers excellent resistance to scratches and UV light.
- **Glossy Finish**: Enhances the appearance of printed graphics.
- **Easy Application**: Designed for quick and easy installation on flat surfaces.

## Recommended Uses
- Vehicle wraps
- Signage
- Wall graphics
- Any application requiring protection of printed media

## Shelf Life
3M Scotchcal Overlaminates have a shelf life of 2 years when stored properly in the original packaging, away from direct sunlight and excessive humidity.
