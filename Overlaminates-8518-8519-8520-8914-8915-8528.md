# 3M Scotchcal Overlaminates

## Product Overview
A comprehensive line of protective overlaminates designed to enhance and extend the life of 3M graphics. Each variant offers unique characteristics for different applications and environmental conditions.

---

## Product Line

### 8518 - Gloss Overlaminate
**Key Features:**
- Provides a wet paint look finish
- Highly conformable
- Resists fuel vapors
- Suitable for windows and glass

**Recommended Uses:**
- Fleet/vehicle graphics
- Vehicle wraps
- Watercraft
- Transit graphics
- Labels
- Thermoformable graphics
- Smooth walls
- Windows and glass

**Performance:**
- Expected performance life: 8 years (vertical, outdoor surfaces)
- Non-warranty period applied to flat, vertical, outdoor surfaces
