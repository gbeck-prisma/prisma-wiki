# 3M Envision Print Wrap Film LX480mC and SV480mC

## Product Description
3M Envision Print Wrap Film LX480mC and SV480mC are high-performance wrap films that provide excellent conformability, durability, and print quality. Designed for vehicle graphics and other demanding applications, these films offer a combination of aesthetic appeal and performance.

## Features
- **Exceptional Print Quality**: Designed to deliver vibrant colors and high-resolution graphics.
- **Conformability**: Easily conforms to complex surfaces and curves.
- **Durability**: Provides long-lasting performance in both indoor and outdoor settings.
- **Easy to Install**: Features an air-release adhesive for easy handling and installation.
- **Environmental Sustainability**: Made with sustainable materials and a focus on reducing environmental impact.

## Recommended Uses
- Vehicle wraps and graphics
- Signage in indoor and outdoor environments
- Fleet graphics
- Promotional displays

## Physical and Application Characteristics
- Finish: Gloss or matte
- Thickness: 2-mil (50 micron)
- Adhesive Type: Pressure-sensitive
- Liner: Polycoated liner

## Performance Life
- Expected durability: Up to **12 years** in outdoor applications.
- Indoor applications may last even longer under ideal conditions.

## Compatible Products
- 3M Graphic Film Series
- 3M Adhesives and Sealants
- Compatible with various printers, inks, and lamination products.

## Manufacturing Guidelines
- Ensure surfaces are clean, dry, and smooth before application.
- Use proper tools and techniques to avoid damage during installation.
- Follow 3M guidelines for optimal results.

## Shelf Life
- **2 years** when stored in a controlled environment (70°F to 100°F and 40% to 60% relative humidity).
